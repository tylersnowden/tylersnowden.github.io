Alerion Art - Online Course Storefront

Alerion Art was a partnership with an artist to sell an online art course. It was built with Laravel 8 and a CMS called OctoberCMS. The plugins to sell the product were custom built and was hosted on AWS. Each chapter in the course had a video and progress was tracked for each user. Payment processing was done with a Stripe integration and I managed and split tested Facebook and Instagram ads to drive traffic to the site.

Currently, a static version of the site is hosted on Netlify and the course is no longer for sale.

  • PHP / Laravel
  • OctoberCMS
  • TailwindCSS
  • Stripe

Multiplayer Minesweeper - Made with PartyKit
